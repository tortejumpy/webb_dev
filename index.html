FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How does the playlist generator work? The playlist generator uses an algorithm that takes into account the user's selected mood and preferences to generate a customized playlist.
Can I customize the playlist based on specific genres? Yes, you can customize the playlist by selecting specific genres or by providing keywords related to the desired music style.
Can I save and export the generated playlists? Yes, you can save and export the generated playlists in various formats, such as CSV or JSON.
Is there a limit to the number of songs in a playlist? There is no specific limit to the number of songs in a playlist. However, for optimal performance, we recommend keeping the playlist size reasonable.
Can I share my customized playlists with others? Yes, you can easily share your customized playlists with others by generating a unique link or exporting the playlist in a compatible format.
